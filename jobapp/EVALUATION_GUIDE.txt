1. DATABASE DESIGN (20 MARKS) ✅
   └─ ER Diagram Implementation
      ├─ 5 Tables: Applicant, Submissions, Background, Locate, Visions
      ├─ Primary Keys: ALL tables have AUTO_INCREMENT PK
      ├─ Foreign Keys: ALL tables (except Applicant) have FK with CASCADE delete
      ├─ Normalization: 3NF Compliance (No insertion/update anomalies)
      ├─ NOT NULL constraints on critical fields
